About us

Ligal Aid is a legal tech organization. We provide case reporting and case management software for legal matters handled by individuals and organizations pro bono. Through this platform, individuals who have a legal need but cannot afford to pay for the services of a lawyer can request for legal assistance. Lawyers who are interested in providing pro bono legal assistance to indigents can also sign up on the platform.

We also provide our software for free use by legal aid organizations in Nigeria!

How you can use Ligal Aid to help others.

Find out how you can use Ligal Aid to handle cases, pro bono, and much more. üë©üèæ‚Äç‚öñÔ∏è

  • As a lawyer (Inclusive of Youth Corpers and Law Students)
  • As an Access to Justice/Legal Aid provider, Legal Aid CDS Group or Law Clinic

Are you a lawyer willing to provide your services pro bono? Ligal Aid affords every lawyer an opportunity to do legal work, pro bono, according to their availability.

Via the Ligal Aid platform, lawyers can provide legal counsel and support to indigents either by taking up matters that require physical representation or providing legal support virtually.

When you sign up on the platform, we will verify the information you have provided and (where correct), approve your application. Using the information you have provided, we will assign cases to you, tailored to your experience, expertise and availability. We will also provide back-end support to assist you with the cases you have taken up.

Nigeria has hundreds of Legal Aid CDS groups, scores of organizations in the access to justice sector and over a dozen law clinics across law faculties. These bodies do amazing work by providing indigents with pro bono legal services.

Ligal Aid is here to support your activities by providing its software to help you handle case reporting and case management.

Are you a member or staff of a legal aid organization/Legal Aid CDS group/Law Clinic? Would you like to see your organization use Ligal Aid to take up and manage cases? Reach out to us by sending an email or by simply contacting us!
